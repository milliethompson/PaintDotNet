Red Eye Removal Effect

The Red Eye Removal Effect (Effects-->Red Eye Removal) corrects for Red Eye in digital photographs.  Removal operates best with a minimal selection made around the affected portion.  This portion of the layer must be selected using either the Rectangle Select or the Lasso Select.

The Red Eye Removal effect can better perform red eye reduction through optimization of the tolerance and saturation sliders.  The image to the right demonstrates the dialog with its default values.
