Background / Foreground Color Selection Tool

The Background and Foreground Color Selection Tools are located at the bottom of the Tools Window.  For instance, in the picture to the left, the foreground color is blue and the background color is red.  In order to change the colors, simply click the color that you want to change and the Color Window will pop-up.  Use this window to change to the desired color. 

A user may click on the double-headed arrows to swap colors between foreground and background. Clicking the small black box above the white box will restore the original default colors of a black foreground and a white background.
