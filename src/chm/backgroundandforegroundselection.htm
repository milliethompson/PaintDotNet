Background / Foreground Color Selection Tool

The Background and Foreground Color Selection Tools are located at the bottom of the Tools Window.  For instance, in the picture to the left, the foreground color is blue and the background color is red.  In order to change the colors, simple click which color you want to change and the Color Window will pop-up.  Use this window to change to the desired color.  There are two more features as well.  The first one is the double-headed arrow.  Use this to swap colors between the foreground and the background.  The second extra feature, which resembles the black box on top of white box, sets the colors back to their defaults of black and white.
