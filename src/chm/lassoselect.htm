Lasso Select

The Lasso Select Tool (Tools-->Lasso Select) is one of the primary methods in which you can select a piece of the graphic for either modification, deletion, or copying.  The use of this tool is rather simple. Click on the Lasso Select Tool, and then on the active layer click and drag the mouse around the area you would like selected.  Once you have the area you would like, let go of the mouse button.  This tool can be used in conjunction with the Rectangle Select Tool by holding down the ctrl key. 

 

 

 
The picture to the left is a region currently selected with the Lasso Selection Tool. 
Important:  Once a selection region (or regions) are created, you may only alter that region.  This includes any and all alterations.  For instance, if selection regions are made, the Pencil Tool will only work in those regions until those regions are unselected.  To deselect a region, ensure the Lasso Tool (or Rectangle Select) tool is activated, click on a region without dragging, and the the selection region will be cancelled.  You may also click Edit --> Deselect to deselect everything. Then you may continue working on the entire canvas as a whole.
