Resize Operation

The Resize Operation (Image-->Resize) comes in two flavors: Nearest Neighbor and Bicubic.  Once you select the Resize Operation, you will be presented with a new window.  In this window, you can set whether or not to keep the aspect ratio, select one of the above flavors of resize, and input the new dimensions for the entire image.  If you choose not to keep the aspect ratio, expect the new image to show some level of distortion.

Also, notice that the Bicubic resize differs greatly from the Nearest Neighbor resize.  The Nearest Neighbor resize has more of a Mosaic look to it, while the Bicubic resize appears somewhat blurry.
