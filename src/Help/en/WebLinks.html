Web Links

Paint.NET Website: http://www.eecs.wsu.edu/paint.net

This is the best place to go to download Paint.NET, or to get information about Paint.NET.

Paint.NET Forum: http://paintdotnet.forumer.com

This is the best place to go for asking questions on how to use Paint.NET, to report bugs, or to ask most questions.

Paint.NET Contact E-mail: paint.net@hotmail.com (English and German)

It is appropriate to send bug reports, general feedback, and licensing and distribution questions to this e-mail address. For "how to" type questions, please post on the forum.

Rick Brewster's Technical Blog: http://blogs.msdn.com/rickbrew

Rick is the lead developer on Paint.NET. At his blog you will find discussion about Paint.NET, C# and threading performance, and news about upcoming Paint.NET features and release dates.
