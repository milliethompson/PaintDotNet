Zoom Operations

The Zoom feature (View-->Zoom In, View--> Zoom Out) allows the image to be viewed in greater or less detail by enlarging or shrinking the view of the image. Zoom in will increase the zoom by two each time, zoom out will reduce the zoom by one half each time. To show the actual size selected View -> Actual Size. Also, the zoom may be set to keep a given region in view, either a selection or the entire image by clicking on "Zoom to Window" or "Zoom to Selection".

Also, the zoom adjustments are accessible on the toolbar as shown on the right. These buttons allow you to zoom in, zoom out, or specify an arbitrary zoom.
