Hue / Saturation

The Hue and Saturation of an image may be adjusted using Layers --> Adjustments --> Hue / Saturation. This operation may be used to change the saturation of colors, as well as rotate the hue of the image through the rainbow, additionally, it allows the adjustment of lightness.

Shown on the right is the Hue / Saturation dialog. When the sliders are moved, the image will be updated giving a preview of the final result of this operation.
