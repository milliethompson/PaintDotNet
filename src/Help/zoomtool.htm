Zoom Tool

The Zoom Tool (Tools -->Zoom) allows for arbitrary zoom, general zooming in towards a target, or for panning. Left click and drag to zoom in to a particular area, or left click without drag to zoom in on a partiular region. Right clicking will always zoom out. To pan using the zoom tool, use your middle mouse button and move in the direction you wish to pan.
